Who we are

We are a nomadic group of economically accessible pharmacology consultants using the synaptic priming framework to improve clinical care for psychiatric disorders.

What we do

Primer pharmacology

  • Designing novel antidepressant treatment paradigms that harness the unique pharmacology of ketamine and psychedelics
  • Combining available therapies to accelerate symptom relief, enhance therapeutic effects, and prolong remission
  • Reducing patient and health-care provider burden by refining dosage regimens
